Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Weeka

What is Weeka?

Weeka is a visual weekly planner designed specifically for families. It helps parents and children create clear, visual schedules for activities, treats, and routines throughout the week. By using colorful icons and simple interfaces, even non-reading children can understand and follow their weekly plans.

How does Weeka help reduce daily negotiations?

By establishing a visual weekly routine that everyone can see and understand, Weeka eliminates the constant "Can I have...?" questions. Children know exactly when they'll have screen time, treats, or special activities, reducing anxiety and creating clear expectations for the whole family.

Is Weeka suitable for non-reading children?

Absolutely! Weeka was designed with pre-readers in mind. Each activity is represented by a colorful, intuitive icon that children can easily recognize. This visual approach is inspired by kindergarten planning systems that have been proven effective for young children.

Can I print the weekly calendars?

Yes! Weeka includes a beautiful print feature that creates fridge-ready calendars. You can print weekly views that maintain all the colorful icons and clear layout, making it easy for the whole family to check the schedule throughout the day.

How many family members can use Weeka?

Weeka supports multiple family member profiles, so you can create individual schedules for each child while maintaining an overview of the entire family's week. Each child can have their own personalized activities and routines.

Can I customize the activity icons?

Currently, Weeka offers a comprehensive set of pre-designed icons for common activities like sleep, meals, school, sports, screen time, and treats. We're constantly adding new icons based on user feedback to cover more activities.

Is Weeka free to use?

Weeka offers a free version with core features including weekly planning, printing, and basic themes. Premium features may be available in the future for families who want additional customization options.

What devices does Weeka work on?

Weeka is currently available as an iOS app for iPhone and iPad. The app is optimized for both devices, with the iPad version offering an expanded view perfect for family planning sessions.

How do recurring activities work?

When creating an activity, you can set it to repeat weekly, making it easy to establish consistent routines. For example, if swimming lessons are every Tuesday at 4 PM, you only need to set it up once, and it will appear every week automatically.

Is my family's data private and secure?

Absolutely! Weeka is completely local-first and open source. Your family's data never leaves your device unless you choose to sync it with your personal iCloud account. We don't collect any data, require no accounts, and have no servers. You can verify this yourself by reviewing our open source code. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Is Weeka really open source?

Yes! Weeka is fully open source. You can view our entire codebase on GitHub, contribute improvements, or even fork it to create your own version. We believe in transparency and giving users complete control over their tools.
